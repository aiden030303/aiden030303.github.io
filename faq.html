Frequently asked questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
  1. Access the HZ website and sign in with your HZ credentials.
  2. Upload your document by selecting "Choose File." Add more files if needed.
  3. Check the "Advanced" box to pick double-sided or black-and-white options.
  4. Select either the "HZ printer" or "HZ plotter" for your print job.
  5. At the printer, scan your HZ pass on the TouchID and choose "Print."
  6. Pick the printer you sent the job to from the menu.
  7. Ensure your printing account has enough credit to complete the job.
  8. After printing, press "Stop" on the TouchID, then select "Logout."
How do you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
  1. Visit any HZ printer and scan your HZ card.
  2. Place your document on the scanner bed or in the feeder.
  3. Select the scan option and choose your laptop as the destination.
  4. Check your HZ email or designated folder for the scanned file.
How do you buy something, like signing up for the IT introduction event, on the HZ web shop?
  1. Visit The HZ shop and log in with your HZ account.
  2. Browse events or items, like the IT introduction event.
  3. Add your selection to the cart and proceed to checkout.
  4. Pay using iDEAL or credit card and confirm your purchase.
How do you book a project space?
  1. Access the Self Service Portal at portal HZ with your HZ account.
  2. Navigate to the room booking section.
  3. Select an available project space in your desired wing.
  4. Confirm the date, time, and duration, then submit your booking.
How do you park your car at the HZ parking lot?
  1. Drive to the PZEM parking lot at Poelendaelesingel 10, Middelburg.
  2. Show your HZ pass at the barrier to enter.
  3. Park in the designated university lot.
  4. Ensure your pass is visible to avoid fines.
How do you use the coffee machine on campus?
  1. Locate the coffee machine in the HZ cafeteria or common area.
  2. Insert your payment card or use contactless payment.
  3. Select your drink (e.g., espresso, cappuccino) on the screen.
  4. Wait 20-30 seconds for your drink to dispense.
