My HBO-ICT Choice

September 5th 2025

I chose the HBO-ICT program at HZ University of Applied Sciences because it aligns with my passion for technology. Since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by computers, from building my own to exploring how websites and software work. The joy of solving problems through coding drives me veen though ive just started two weeks ago. Below, I share my journey to choosing this program, why it motivates me, and my goals after graduation.

Study Choice Activities

I explored my study options through practical steps. I attended an HZ open day in 2024 to learn about the HBO-ICT program. I also researched online, visiting hz.nl to get fimiliar with the curriculum and tracks like Software Engineering and Data Science. These activities confirmed HBO-ICT as my top choice.

Motivation for HBO-ICT

Computers have always excited me. Problem-solving is my favorite part—tackling a coding issue, over the past 2 weeks finding a solution to a problem ive been frustrated with for ages feels rewarding. HBO-ICT suits me because it emphasizes practical skills, like developing apps or designing systems. With two parents who both worked in IT, computer science seemed like the natural descision for me, but after the past two weeks im sure i made the right descision.

After Completing the Study

After graduating, I would like to start my own company in software developemnt or cyber security. Both these profressions appeal to me because they seem fun and comfortable. I want to build user-friendly websites or protect systems from threats. The ICT field offers diverse roles in wich im sure i'll find something that suits me.

Conclusion

Choosing HBO-ICT at HZ was a natural decision. My love for technology and problem-solving fits the program’s practical approach. I’m excited to grow my skills and build a career in ICT.
